Budgeting Basics

Learn how to create and stick to a budget.

  1. Assess your income: Calculate your total monthly earnings.
  2. Track your expenses: Record all spending for a month.
  3. Categorize spending: Divide expenses into needs and wants.
  4. Set goals: Decide how much to save or spend in each category.
  5. Create your budget: Use a 50/30/20 rule or customize.
  6. Monitor and adjust: Review weekly and tweak as needed.

Understanding Credit

Explore how credit works and how to manage it.

  1. Learn key terms: Understand credit scores, APR, and limits.
  2. Check your credit report: Request a free report annually.
  3. Understand credit impact: See how payments affect your score.
  4. Use credit wisely: Keep balances low and pay on time.
  5. Fix mistakes: Dispute errors on your credit report.
